Interactive Registry

This map displays the detections of DeepPVMapper. For privacy reasons, we do not disclose the individual panels but only the aggregation at the city level. This map is optimized for desktop browsing.

How to Use the Map

Each point represents a city. When clicking on it, a popup with some statistics regarding the detection and the PV systems will appear.

Available Information

  • Number of detected systems: We follow the same censoring as the RNI and truncate the data at 10 systems per city. If there is no recorded system or no detection, we indicate None.
  • Aggregated installed capacity: Total estimated installed capacity for the city.
  • Detection year: The year the image of that city was taken.

Advanced Features

When there are more than 30 systems in the city, we additionally display the distribution of the installed capacity, tilt angles and azimuth angles of the PV systems in that city.

Consensus Metrics

We indicate some statistics quantifying the consensus between DeepPVMapper and the RNI. We display the pseudo precision, recall and F1 score, indicating how aligned DeepPVMapper and the RNI in terms of estimation of the installed capacity at the scale of the department.

Whenever available, we also indicate the pseudo average percentage error, which indicate how congruent both sources are within the considered city.

We refer the reader to our main paper for more details regarding the construction and the interpretation of the different metrics.
