DeepPVMapper

460,000+ raw rooftop PV detections across France by deep learning · ≈ 2.3 GWp

Navigate

Zoom to change scale: régions, then départements, then communes appear under your cursor, with their detection counts. Click a zone to lock onto it — detailed statistics open, and you can zoom all the way down to individual installations. Click anywhere outside the zone to unlock. The search bar jumps straight to any place.

Contribute

Spotted an error? On any detected installation: report a false detection or redraw its outline. Missed installation? Use the Add installation button and draw it. Submissions are reviewed and merged into future releases of the registry.

Data

This map shows the raw detections — unfiltered model output, which is exactly why your corrections matter. The registry is hosted on cartes.gouv.fr (IGN Géoplateforme) and served live via WFS — public access coming soon. Detections are produced by the DeepPVMapper pipeline from IGN aerial orthoimagery.
